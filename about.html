What We Do

Six Core Service Lines

Case Screening

Initial evaluation of case files to assess viability, documentation completeness, and action priority for your legal team.

Medical Records Review

Systematic review of medical records to identify key facts, treatment timelines, and documentation gaps relevant to the case.

Summary Preparation

Clear, structured summaries of complex medical and legal records — written for attorney comprehension, not clinical jargon.

Claim File Indexing

Organized indexing of workers' compensation claim files so your attorneys can locate critical documents instantly.

Chronology Services
